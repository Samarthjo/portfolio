Hi, Welcome to the daily life of Samarth

Navigating Life with Passion and Purpose:My Journey

Life has a way of shaping us through experiences, environments, and people who influence our path in unexpected ways. My story is no different. Born in Bhopal, India, and raised in Bahrain from the age of one, I grew up in an atmosphere that blended cultural diversity and deep-rooted values. The 18 years I spent in Bahrain were not only formative but also created the foundation for both my personal and professional identity.It gave me new sense of looking hings differntly and from various perspectives.

A Childhood Rich in Connections and Curiosity

From a young age, I was surrounded by a loving community of Indian neighbors who made Bahrain feel like home. Coming from a middle-income family, my childhood was filled with simple joys and strong values. Sports played a huge role in those early years — basketball, volleyball, chess, and even traditional Indian games like carrom. They didn’t just keep me active; they taught me teamwork, patience, and resilience. Video games, particularly on my console, were my go-to stress relievers during high school. Gaming wasn’t just a pastime; it allowed me to think critically and unwind after a long day of studies and social interactions. But, as life goes on, priorities change. The Covid-19 pandemic brought about a shift in my interests. Social media started taking up more space in my life, and I lost some of the connections I once cherished. However, the period of isolation also gave me time to reflect and focus on the kind of person I am—soft-hearted, empathetic, and someone who values logical thinking above all else.

Shaping My Professional Path: From Commerce to Business Analytics

Academically, I’ve always sought knowledge that would challenge me and push me towards growth. After completing my schooling at The Asian School in Bahrain, I moved to India to pursue higher education. In my 10th grade, I chose commerce with math, a decision driven by my interest in understanding how businesses operate from a quantitative perspective. My passion for logical thinking naturally led me to join Christ University in Bangalore, where I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) with a specialization in Business Analytics. Now in my 5th semester, I find myself drawn to the world of data-driven decision-making and analytics. The field offers a unique intersection of logic, strategy, and technology, which aligns perfectly with my personal and professional interests. I’ve come to appreciate how important it is to make informed, data-backed decisions in today's dynamic business environment.
